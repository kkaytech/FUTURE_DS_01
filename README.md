# Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, high-value categories, and regional performance.

The goal is to derive actionable insights that can help a business improve profitability and make data-driven decisions.

##  Objectives
- Analyze sales trends over time  
- Identify top-selling products  
- Evaluate category-wise performance  
- Assess regional profitability  
- Provide business recommendations  

##  Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)  
- Power BI  
- Jupyter Notebook  

##  Dataset
- Superstore Sales Dataset (Kaggle)  
- Includes sales, profit, category, region, and product data  

## Key Analysis Performed
- Data cleaning and preprocessing  
- KPI calculation (Sales, Profit, Profit Margin)  
- Trend analysis (monthly/quarterly sales)  
- Category and regional performance analysis  
- Product-level performance evaluation  

##  Key Insights
- Technology category generated the highest revenue among all categories  
- West region showed the highest profitability compared to other regions  
- Sales showed an upward trend during Q4, indicating seasonal demand  
- Some categories had high sales but relatively low profit margins  

##  Recommendations
- Focus marketing and inventory efforts on high-performing categories like Technology  
- Re-evaluate pricing or discount strategies in low-profit regions  
- Leverage seasonal trends to maximize Q4 revenue  
- Optimize underperforming product segments
